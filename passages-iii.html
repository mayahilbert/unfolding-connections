Joyce Yu-Jean Lee: Unfolding Connections

Passages III

2012-2020

Digital photo prints, 18” x 24”

Passages III is the third installment of the artist's photographic series, which catalogues scenes from her travels in China and Hong Kong, to family homes in Taiwan and finally to a mandated quarantine in New York City. Inspired by Chinese inhabitants carrying out their daily lives, the artist discreetly records their presence as a candid travelogue. In addition to her family's own diasporic journey, the photographic series also reveals the artist's interest in oculi and their ability to both shape and direct light as it moves from one space into another. Passages III presents an evolving perspective on transnational identity across international locations. Passages III continues to shape the artist's own understanding of the complexity and fluidity of identity.
