Joyce Yu-Jean Lee: Unfolding Connections

What It's Like, What It Is

2022

Video animation, 1:00:01 looped

A text-based animation made in collaboration and solidarity with the Asian American community in NYC. What It's Like, What It Is was developed when violence against Asian-Americans erupted at the start of the Covid-19 pandemic. Made in homage to Adrian Piper's video installation, "What Itâ€™s Like, What It Is #3 (1991)," a two-channel video displays words submitted by members of the community: both derogatory and stereotyping words that strangers applied to Asian-Americans, presented alongside positive words that reframe, redefine and reclaim their collective identity.
